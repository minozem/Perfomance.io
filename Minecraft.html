The storyline

In this game you are given complete freedom of action. Now you will not be building alone, but looking for companions. It will be easier and more fun this way. In the game you will create square structures of various shapes. First, you will build houses out of cubes, and then you can build beautiful buildings - here show your gaming imagination and imagination. You have to learn a lot of crafts as the game progresses, but spend more time searching for valuable resources. Underground, you will have a chance to find various artifacts. Ore, wood and gold can be used to make unusual weapons for yourself. Also, just move around the virtual square world and enjoy the buildings created by your character. For each successfully completed game stage, you will receive bonuses to develop the skills of your cubic hero. Also, during the game you have to take care of your garden - you can grow a variety of crops there.

Game mechanics

Now your main character will even be able to fly, because this game has added wings for flying. But your buildings need to be constantly upgraded. To do this, you must learn how to place square blocks correctly in order to create unique structures in game locations. In the Minecraft game, which can be downloaded for free on our game server, you can play in single player mode, or you can try the cooperative mode and work with friends to build, search for resources, tame animals. The cubic game world is limitless, so use your creative abilities to create real game building masterpieces.

The epilogue

Being transported into such an unusual game universe, you can do anything you want. But do not forget about the dangerous enemies that you will encounter throughout all the game missions. Good luck to you, digger and builder!
