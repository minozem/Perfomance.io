Marvels Spider Man Miles Morales

  • Publisher: Insomniac Games
  • Developer: Sony Interactive Entertainment
  • Year of release: 2020
  • Category: Action-adventure
  • Interface language: Russian, English
  • Voice-over language: Russian, English

The storyline

The plot starts about a year after the completion of the first part. Miles, under the supervision of his friend and mentor Peter Parker, learns the superhero craft and defends Harlem, where he recently moved with his mother, Rio Morales. A helicopter carrying a Rhino and other criminals crashes in the city. As a result of the chase, Parker loses consciousness, but the young hero manages to defeat the Rhino with the help of an unexpectedly discovered ability to control electricity. After the battle, the mercenaries of the Roxon Corporation, led by Simon Krieger, arrive and take the Rhino. This is how we are introduced to the main antagonist of the game.

Game mechanics

Marvel’s Spider-Man featured Peter Parker as the iconic superhero, but and while Miles Morales takes up the job as the wall-crawler in the upcoming PS5 and PS4 title, his combat style is different to Parker’s. As such, this video looks at how the team has refined combat to fit with Morales’ abilities.

The epilogue

As already mentioned, this is an independent offshoot of the original game. Thus, the visual part remains the same, but new areas of research, local tasks and new ones are emerging. Despite the fact that the atmosphere associated with Miles has always been friendly and not so serious, the developers decided to clarify and make the new Spider-Man feel the full burden of responsibility that Peter Parker went through. Spider-Man: Miles Morales uses technology developed by Insomniac Games itself. The game engine has been redesigned to take advantage of the PlayStation 5. On PlayStation 5, the game works not only in 4K resolution with ray tracking and 3D sound, but can also be run in a special mode at a speed of 60 images per second. In addition, the game uses the unique capabilities of the dualsense controller, that is, tactile vibrations and adaptive triggers, and also uses SSD capabilities (this is mainly manifested in almost instantaneous loading time).
